X3BANNERS

REQUIREMENTS: none
---
WHAT IT DOES: show a banner on top of a page
---
HOW IT WORKS:
- you can create one or more banners for each page with different colors and texts with a defined time window
- if one page have more than one active banner the system will pick one randomly
